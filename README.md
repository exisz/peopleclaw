# 🐾 PeopleClaw

People-centric workflow automation for SMEs, small businesses & freelancer ITs.

## What is PeopleClaw?

PeopleClaw is a workflow-centric platform where:
- **SMEs & small businesses** define their business workflows visually
- **Freelancer ITs** build and configure automations for their clients
- **AI assists** at every step, but **humans approve** at key decision points

Part of the OpenClaw ecosystem.
